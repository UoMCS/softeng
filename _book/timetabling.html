Weekly timetable

The weekly schedule for autumn 2021 is shown in table 0.1, based on timetables.manchester.ac.uk, see also manchester.ac.uk/discover/key-dates key dates.

  • Other than the introductory lecture in week 1, there are no lectures. Instead we have workshops which are more like labs and may contain mini-lectures
  • Workshops are on Tuesday or Friday afternoon depending on your lab group
  • Team study sessions are on Tuesdays at 10am and Thursday at 11am
Table 0.1: The weekly schedule for this twelve week course, please note we are using the week numbering from the timetables.manchester.ac.uk where week zero is welcome week, and week one is the first teaching week
Week no. Subject Deadlines
0: 20th Sept Configuring, see section 0.10
1: 27th Sept Automated build and test, see see section 0.11 IndCwk2, Fri 1st Oct, 6pm
2: 4th Oct Reading large codebases, see see section 0.12
3: 11th Oct Debugging, see see section 0.13 IndCwk2, Fri 15th Oct, 6pm
4: 18th Oct Cost estimation, see see section 0.14
5: 25th Oct Test first development, see see section 0.15 TeamCwk1, 29th Oct, 6pm
6: 1st Nov Reading week see see section 0.16
7: 8th Nov Git workflows, see see section 0.17
8: 15th Nov Software refactoring, see see section 0.18
9: 22nd Nov Design for testability, see see section 0.19
